With unmatched biodiversity, breathtaking volcanoes, and bustling metropolises,

the Philippines is one of the gems of the western Pacific Ocean.

There are 3142 named summits in the Philippines.

The highest and most prominent mountain is Mount Apo (2,954 m/9,692 ft).

The Philippines (Pilipinas), or the Republic of the Philippines (Republika ng Pilipinas),

is an Asian country and archipelago consisting of 7,641 islands.

The Philippines covers an area of roughly 300,000 sq km (120,000 sq mi),

making it the seventy-second largest country in the world.
